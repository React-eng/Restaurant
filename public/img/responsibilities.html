Responsibilities

  • For 1 entree , serve half portion of rice. ( Small White container )
  • For 2 entree, serve full portion of rice ( Big containers with silver lids)
  • Ask for mild, medium or spicy levels before entering the ticket
  • From Monday to friday( untill 3 p.m ) for the buffet menu ask the customer how many combo items?
  • For combos grab containers on the shelf start with rice and add genuine amount of gravy. ( If garlic naan is needed add garlic for 1$ on the main menu on tab)
  • Buffet generally contains Chicken curry, Veg. Saag, Navratna Korma and Chiken Tikka Masala
  • Ask for softdrinks and charge the ticket.
  • You also have to pack the to-go food in plastic bags. ( Be attentive to chef while packing and name the respective containers using markers )
  • Phone orders can be accepted when the Restaurant is not too busy otherwise the owner would receive the ticket and print it directly.
  • The tickets are supposed to be pasted on the top of the counter using tape. ( Note the orders of tickets )
  • If the tickets have samosa, paneer pakora or tyga bites start deep frying them. ( when done add Chat masala, chutneys or sauces )
  • For 1 order of samosa take 2 pieces and serve in paper bag, for more than 2 samosas use container.
  • Do not forget to stick the ticket on to-go bags once order is finised as well as placing them on a table
  • Always ask for Name for to-go orders and check if the order was paid for.
  • Customers are allowed to sit and eat inside but dont forget to clean the table ASAP.
  • For instore customers- Welcome them, give them menu cards and help them with menu. Once order is finalized ask "for here" / "to-go"
  • If for here- then serve food in tray, Naan in plate, give them utensils and plates.
  • Desserts can be found inside the Cold storage on your left
